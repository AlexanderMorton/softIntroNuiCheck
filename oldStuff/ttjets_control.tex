
Kinematic distributions for reconstructed objects and their comparison to data driven background and MC predictions in the \ttbar + jets cross section measurement, before the unfolding procedure, using the Alpgen+Herwig \ttbar\ MC sample, are shown in Figures~\ref{fig:appttjets_control_jetPt},~\ref{fig:appttjets_control_jet0Pt},~\ref{fig:appttjets_control_jet1Pt},~\ref{fig:appttjets_control_jet2Pt},~\ref{fig:appttjets_control_jet3Pt},~\ref{fig:appttjets_control_leppt},~\ref{fig:appttjets_control_lepeta},~\ref{fig:appttjets_control_met} for the electron and muon channels. The background was estimated using simulation and data-driven techniques, as described in Sections~\ref{sec:ttjets_background},~\ref{sec:ttjets_wjets} and~\ref{sec:ttjets_qcd}. The event selection follows the procedure described in Section~\ref{sec:ttjets_selection}.
